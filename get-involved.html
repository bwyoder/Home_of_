How can you help?

  • Pray! Whether you can donate money or not, you can pray. This is the most powerful thing you can do.

  • Visit. Meet the children, volunteer in the kitchen, and see the beautiful country of Honduras. For more information send and email to info@homeofblessing.org

  • Donate! Home Of Blessing is supported by a wide variety of people who really care about these children in Honduras who are at risk. Join in and donate through the PayPal button on the bottom of this page. Choose which fund you would like to donate to. Set up a monthly recurring payment to make your life simpler.
